CliniquePlus Pay

CliniquePlus Pay is CliniquePlus EMR Software's secure payment. Patients can make personal contributions to their healthcare or receive support/donations from families, friends & well-wishers through dedicated wallets with links that can be shared and payments made. Others can contribute to their medical bills, too. For care practice business, it enables seamless disbursements to staff, vendors or patient from the CliniquePlus EMR Software leveraging on the existing approval workflows for secure funds transfer.
